21/06/1921

Two Sic Mundus followers are digging the time travel passage in Winden's caves, they are young Noah and another guy. Sic Mundus leader Adam warned Noah this unnamed character had lost his faith in the cult, Noah kills him following Adam's orders.

Before being murdered this guy says: "I hope eventually the day will come, when you won't believe everything Adam tells you. You should ask yourself why he took you in, and why he chose to call you Noah."

Later adult Noah will praise him, saying that he did the right thing. Young Noah will ask him why Adam chose him, which will be met with "You will understand when the time will come."

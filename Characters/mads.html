Mads Nielsen

Mads Nielsen was the son of Jana and Tronte Nielsen and brother of Ulrich. Mads disappeared in 1986 at the age of 12. He walked back home through the forest after walking Regina Tiedemann to her house because she was afraid of his brother Ulrich and Katharina. Regina was documented as the last person to see him alive in the police investigation.

He was taken by Helge Doppler and used to test the time machine in the bunker, but died in the process. His body is found on November 5th 2019.

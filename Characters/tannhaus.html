H.G. Tannhaus

Tannhaus is the watchmaker. He is the author of the book "A Journey Through Time". In 1986 adult Jonas pays visit to him and asks him about his book, einstein-rosen bridges and time. Jonas then tells him that his theories are correct, that wormholes exist, in fact there is one in Winden, and that he comes from the future by using it. He gives him the time machine that he built, saying it's broken and that he needs to fix it.

He already had the time machine, which Claudia asked him to buil in 1953, the machine didn't work and he was able to fix it only thanks to seeing the future time machine.

In 1953 adult Ulrich comes by his shop holding the book that he will write as an old man. Ulrich forgets his jacket there that he inspects finding a smartphone

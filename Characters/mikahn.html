Michael is Jonas' father, married to Hannah Kruger. Born as Mikkel, he is the youngest son of Ulrich and Katharina Nielsen.

In 2019, at the age of 11, he unwillingly traveled back in time to 1986 using Winden's caves passsage. Not knowing his way back, he is adopted by Ines Kahnwald and begins a new life as Michael Kahnwald.

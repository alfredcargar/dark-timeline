Michael Kahnwald

Michael is Jonas' father, married to Hannah Kruger. Born as Mikkel, he is the youngest son of Ulrich and Katharina Nielsen.

21/06/2019

Michael kills himself without an apparent reason, he left a letter that says "do not open before November 4th, 10:13 pm". His adoptive mother, Ines, finds it and conserves it for the mentioned date.

On the 4th of November 2019, at the age of 11, he unwillingly traveled back in time to 1986 using the Winden Caves passsage. Not knowing his way back, he is adopted by Ines Kahnwald and begins a new life as Michael Kahnwald.

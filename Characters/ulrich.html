2019

Ulrich is an inspector at Winden's police department, he is Katharina's husband and father of Martha, Magnus and Mikkel. He is secretly having an affair with Hannah Kahnwald. On November 5th, after Mikkel's disappearence, he investigates the caves and finds a locked door to the nuclear plant. After telling the chief of police Charlotte Doppler and asking her for a warrant, he heads to the power plant to ask the director Aleksander Tiedemann to check the area, but he refuses.

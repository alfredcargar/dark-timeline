Ulrich is an inspector at Winden's police department, he is Katharina's husband and father of Martha, Magnus and Mikkel. He is secretly having an affair with Hannah Kahnwald.

On November 5th, after MIkkel's disappearence, he investigates the caves and finds a door to the nuclear plant. He asks the director, Aleksander Tiedemann, to check the area but he refuses.

After following an investigative clue he arrives at Oberman's house, he only finds Erik's stack of drugs.

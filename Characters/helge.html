Helge Doppler

Old

Helge is Peter's father. In 2019 he is an old man apparently senile, he keeps repeating "It's gonna happen again.", "It needs to be stopped.", "I have to stop Noah".

He escapes from the hospital and travels back to 1986 to face his adult self. He confronts him saying that everything Noah says is a lie, they are not the chosen ones. He says Noah is pure evil, he tries to persuade him to stop but adult Helge runs away in shock.

On November 13th 1986 he tries to kill him in a car accident, but dies instead.

In 1953 he is a kid, he takes math lessons from young Claudia Tiedemann.

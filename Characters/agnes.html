Agnes Nielsen

1953

Agnes is Ulrich's grandmother. She said her husband is dead and that he was a priest, but not exactly a man of faith (Noah?).

She is Adam's follower and a member of Sic Mundus.

She meets with Noah in the church, she tells him Claudia has the missing pages in exchange for her to be included again, before the new cicle begins. She tells him "you wouldn't say no to your own sister would you?".

1921

Agnes kills Noah following Adam's orders.

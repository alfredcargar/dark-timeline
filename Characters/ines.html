Ines Kahnwald

Ines Kahnwald is the adoptive mother of Michael Kahnwald. In 1986 she takes care of Mikkel after he came from the future and ended up adopting him. Growing old she will eventually start believing Mikkel is from the future and will realize the truth after reading the letter on November 4th 2019.

When Jonas comes to talk with her she shows him the letter, much to his surprise since he believed to have burnt it. She reveals to Jonas that he always knew Mikkel came from the future and that it had happened for a reason they are not yet to know.

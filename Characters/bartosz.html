Bartosz Tiedemann

2019

Bartosz is Aleksander and Regina's son. He is Jonas best friend. While Jonas was in treatment for depression due to his father's suicide, he covered for him saying he was in an exchange program in France.

On November 4th he persuades his friends to go to the caves to find Erik Obendorf's drugs.

Some days after he receives a call from Noah, thinking he is Erik's supplier he decides to meet up with him. Noah will tell him exactly what will happen in the next few days, this will be enough for Bartosz to trust Noah's word.

On November 11 old Claudia pays him a visit, he is suprised because he thought she was dead. She leaves him with a picture to give his mother.

2020

On June 24, Bartosz comes out of the caves carrying a time machine. He is surprised by Magnus, Martha and Franziska, who tie him up and leave with the time machine. They return the day after demanding explanations, he finally says the device is a time machine, they force him to prove so he activates it taking them back to 1987

Jonas Kahnwald

Teen

Jonas Kahnwald is the son of Michael and Hannah Kahnwald. As a teenager, he is traumatized by his father's suicide, for which he seeks therapy with Peter Doppler during the summer.

04/11/2019

Jonas goes along with his friends to the caves, searching for Erik's stack of drugs. After the loud noises coming from within, he runs away holding Mikkel's arm, but trips and loses sight of him. He has a vision of his father covered in dark liquid and runs away.

05/11/2019

He visits the attic where his father killed himself, there he finds a detailed map of Winden Caves hidden in the ceiling.

07/11/2019

Jonas meets his adult self at the graveyard. He had Regina delivered to him a package with some tools to explore the caves, including a detailed map, and his father's suicide letter.

08/11/2019

Jonas explores the caves and finds the Sic Mundus passage. He opens the metal door and goes through it, meanwhile all lights in Winden start flickering. He comes accross a fork and turns right, then after another metal door with the Sic Mundus incisions he exits from the cave in 1986.

08/11/1986

Jonas comes out of the caves and comes across his mother, still a child, in a van with her father. They stop by since its raining heavily and offer him a ride, but he refuses and walks away.

09/11/1986

He goes to Winden's school and finds Regina Tiedemann, asks her about Michael Kahnwald, she says she only knows Ines Kahnwald and he could find her at the hospital. On the way back he is stopped by Egon Tiedemann, he lies to him about being hurt and is given a ride to the hospital.

At the hospital he finds his grandmother Ines, she is with Mikkel. Before he could do anything his adult self stops him, saying that if he were to take Mikkel back then he would erase his own existance. He goes back to 2019 that same night and burns the letter that was given to him.

10/11/2019

Jonas goes to talk to his grandmother, she gives him the letter he thought he had burnt. After talking with Ines he goes back to 1986 . In the hospital he finds Noah in Mikkel's room, and is kidnapped by him and Helge. He wakes up in the bunker room, where adult Jonas will explain him who he is. He also tells him that free will is an illusion.

After his adult self activates the time machine in the Sic Mundus passage, he sees a breach in time and young Hegel on the other side. They touch hands and he is sent in the bunker in 2052.

In 2052 he is captured by Elizabeth and almost executed, but freed by a girl in exchange for showing her what's in the forbidden zone that he had entered. Jonas takes her there and shows her the god particle, that he knows how to activate because of Claudia's tape recordings.

Jonas travels to 1921 using the god particle. After being taken care of by some folks he meets young Noah, he was somehow expecting him. He goes back to the caves only to find out the passage is not built yet, so he's stuck in 1921. Young Noah was waiting outside for him and takes him to Sic Mundus hideout. Inside the church adult Noah approaches them, he then takes him to Adam. He will explain who he is and that Sic Mundus wants to create a world where god and time don't exist. He says that time is an infinite loop and that everything is going to repeat itself. He then shows to him a wormhole with which he'll travel to 2019.

Adult

In 2019 he meets his teen self, he stays at Regina's hotel and has her send his younger self a package with the Winsten caves map, a torch and his dad suicide letter.

He travels to 1986 to give Tannhaus the broken time machine to fix it. He reveals that he travels through time using a wormhole in Winden, and that the machine can recreate a similar event. He tells the watchmaker that he needs it to destroy the existing wormhole.

He sets the time machine in the Sic Mundus passage and activates it.

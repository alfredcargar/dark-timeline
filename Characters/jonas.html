Jonas Kahnwald is the son of Michael and Hannah Kahnwald. As a teenager, he is traumatized by his father's suicide, for which he seeks therapy with Peter Doppler during the summer.

On November 4th he goes along his friends to the caves, searching for Erik's stack of drugs. After the loud noises coming from within, he runs away holding Mikkel's arm, but trips and lose sight of him. He has a vision of his father covered in dark liquid and runs away.

On November 5th he visits the attic where his father killed himself, there he finds a detailed map of Winden Caves hidden in the ceiling.

Tronte Nielsen

Kid

Tronte is the son of Agnes Nielsen. He arrives at Winden in 1953 with his mother and befriends Claudia Tiedemann. When Agnes goes missing he continues living with the Tiedemann family. One day near the cave he encounters a mysterious man who claim to have given him his name, and gives him the bracalet of a snake eating his own tail, saying it belonged to Agnes.

Adult

Tronte has two kids with Jana, Ulrich and Mads. In 1986 when Mads disappears he isn't home because he is having an affair with Claudia.

Old

In 2019 Peter Doppler calls Tronte to come to the bunker, the body of Mads appeared out of nowhere. Old Claudia soon joins them and explains they have to leave the body where it needs to be found.

Claudia Tiedemann

1986

Claudia is the daughter of Egon and Doris, in 1986 she becomes the nuclear plant new director. The former director, Bernd Doppler, tells her about a nuclear incident in the summer that has been covered up. He also shows her the access to a cave where a number of yellow barrels of nuclear waste are hidden.

She offers a job to young Aleksander, has him weld the door that leads to the cave.

1987

Claudia is visited by her older self, who shows her the time machine and explains that she has to stop a man called Adam in order to save her dughter. Following her older self instructions, se digs up a time-machine from her yard. She will take the apparatus to the clockmaker to have him explain her how it works.

Old

She pays visit to young Bartosz in 2019, giving him a picture to give his mother.

She goes to 1953 to meet O.H. Tannhaus and gives him the blueprint to build a timemachine.

She goes to the bunker when Mads body appears, saying to Peter and Tronte they need to put the body where it needs to be found.

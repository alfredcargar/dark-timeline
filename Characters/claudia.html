Claudia Tiedemann

Young

Claudia is the daughter of Egon and Doris, she is a smart girl and gains some money by helping some kids with their homeworks, like young Helge Doppler. In 1953 they give a room in their house to recently arrived Agnes Nielsen and her son Tronte. Claudia will grow fond of Tronte and start hanging around with him. One day while they were in the woods near, her dog Gretchen disappears into the caves.

Adult

Claudia has a teenage daughter Regina, towards which she does not have much time to dedicate to. She is a single mother, but she has an affair with Tronte. In 1986 she becomes the nuclear plant new director. The former director, Bernd Doppler, tells her about a nuclear incident in the summer that has been covered up. He also shows her the access to a cave where a number of yellow barrels of nuclear waste are hidden. There, she also finds her dog Gretchen, who just disappeared in 1953.

She offers a job to young Aleksander, has him weld the door that leads to the cave.

1987

Claudia is visited by her older self, who shows her the time machine and explains that she has to stop a man called Adam in order to save her dughter Regina. Following her older self instructions, she digs up a time-machine from her yard. She will take the apparatus to the clockmaker to have him explain her how it works.

She then travels to 2020, there she will learn about the past 33 years in the library's archives. She reads she goes missing in 1987 and that her father Egon Tiedemann is going to die under misterious circumstances. When she goes back to 1987 she will go to his house to convince him to come live with her and Regina, but after an argument Egon accidentally hits his head and dies. After the incident she is visited by teen Jonas, who will take her to 2020 again. There she will visit Regina and take her to the bunker.

Post apocalypse

Claudia and Regina survived and are now living in the abandoned police station. One day she returns to the station to find Regina dead.

A few days later, Claudia is visited by herself, from another world, Eva's world. She warns her about Adam, and tells her she has to guide Jonas, and then defy him.

Old

She pays visit to young Bartosz in 2019, giving him a picture to give his mother.

She goes to 1953 to meet O.H. Tannhaus and gives him the blueprint to build a timemachine.

She goes to the bunker when Mads body appears, saying to Peter and Tronte they need to put the body where it needs to be found.

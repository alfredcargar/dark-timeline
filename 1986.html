04/11/1986

Mads Nielson goes missing.

05/11/1986

Mikkel comes out of the cave from 2019.

A herd of 33 sheeps is found dead inexplicably, by Egon Tiedemann and a farmer. In this occasion the latter quotes the bible and tells him: "Parish has a brand new priest, a good man."

Claudia Tiedemann becomes the nuclear plant new director. The ex director shows her the cave where many barrels of nuclear waste are hidden.

07/11/1986

Ulrich is arrested for rape, after Hannah had made up a story about Ulrich assaulting Katharina to Egon Tiedemann.

08/11/1986

Teen Jonas arrives from 2019.

Adult Helge is questioned by Egon on his whereabouts the night Mads disappeared, but avoids answering and postponed the interview, but he will never show. He anyways gives away that that night he took the long road, Egon wrote on his notebook "Why not the forest road?".

09/11/1986

Teen Jonas searches for his grandmother Ines Kahnwald and finds her with Mikkel. Before he could do anything, Adult Jonas intervenes to warn him that if he takes him back, they will never get to exist.

Hegel comes out of the bunker with Yasin, successfully taking him back in time using the chair-time-machine. The boy died in the process. Noah is seen inside the bunker cleaning up and writing in the wall "9.11.1953", just below "5.11.1953" also written by his hand.
